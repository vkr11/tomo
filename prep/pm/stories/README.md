## Mission → Overview → Epic → Story System

This folder implements your narrative structure:

- **Mission**: the umbrella mission or multi-year arc behind a body of work.
- **Overview**: the short top-level narrative you can actually say in an interview before zooming in.
- **Epic**: a multi-quarter slice/workstream inside a mission.
- **Story**: a specific keyframe moment (conflict, tradeoff, failure, insight) you can deliver in STAR.

### How it maps to the rest of the repo

- **Raw inventory**: `users/vikash/background/project_inventory.md`
- **Story strategy**: `prep/framework/10_story_building_strategy.md`
- **Pillar mapping**: `prep/framework/11_story_to_pillar_mapping.md`

### Files

- `index.md`: your master index of missions, overviews, epics, and stories.
- `templates/`: copy/paste templates for new mission/epic/story writeups.
