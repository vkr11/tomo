## Leadership & Culture Principles
Google’s leadership principles are largely informed by "Project Oxygen," an internal research initiative designed to identify what makes managers effective:
* Being a good coach.
* Empowering the team and avoiding micromanagement.
* Showing interest in direct reports' success and well-being.
* Being productive and results-oriented.
* Being a good communicator and an active listener.
* Helping employees with career development.
* Having a clear vision and strategy for the team.
* Having key technical skills to advise the team.

**Core Values & "Ten things we know to be true"**
1. Focus on the user and all else will follow.
2. It’s best to do one thing really, really well.
3. Fast is better than slow.
4. Democracy on the web works.
5. You don’t need to be at your desk to need an answer.
6. You can make money without doing evil.
7. There’s always more information out there.
8. The need for information crosses all borders.
9. You can be serious without a suit.
10. Great just isn’t good enough.

**Culture Pillars:**
- Focus on the User
- Googleyness (Thriving in ambiguity, valuing feedback, challenging the status quo, putting the user first, doing the right thing, caring about the team)
- Psychological Safety & Data-Driven Decision Making

## Interview Process (2025/2026)
1. **Recruiter Screen:** 30 min. Background, fit, compensation.
2. **Phone/Video Screen:** 45 min. Usually with a PM. Focuses on product design, estimation, or rapid-fire behavioral.
3. **Onsite Loop:** 4-6 interviews (45 min each).
   - **Product Design/Sense (1-2 rounds):** Identify user problems, innovation, vision, empathy, prioritization.
   - **Analytical Thinking/Strategy (1-2 rounds):** Insights from ambiguity, estimation, metric deep dives (root cause analysis).
   - **Technical:** Feasibility, system architecture, trade-offs (heavy emphasis for technical PMs).
   - **Behavioral/Leadership/Googleyness:** Conflict, leading without authority, ethics, inclusivity.
   - **Craft & Execution (sometimes):** Agile, project management, constraints.

## Strategy & Context (2025/2026)
- **AI-First:** Gemini integration across Search, Workspace, Cloud. Capital expenditures on custom chips (Ironwood, TPU v5p/v7).
- **Search Shift:** Transitioning to "AI Mode", Deep Research, and Agentic Commerce.
- **Project Astra:** Real-time, context-aware multimodal AI experiences.
