### Weekly Prep Cadence

| Week | Focus | Activities |
|------|-------|------------|
| 1-2 | **Strategy (1) + Sense (2)** | Mock "Design X" + "3-year vision for Y" |
| 3-4 | **Execution (3) + Metrics (4)** | "How would you ship...?" + "Debug this metric drop" |
| 5-6 | **Mgmt (5) + Leadership (6)** | Write 5 stories + practice "influence" & "conflict" |
| 7-8 | **Technical (7) + Domain (8)** | Whiteboard systems + deep-dive domain (Ads/AI/etc.) |
| Ongoing | **Full Loops** | 2x/week mocks across all pillars |

### Prep Prioritization

**Table Stakes (Must be strong)**
*   Strategy (1)
*   Execution (3)
*   Leadership & Drive (6)

**Differentiators (Can win the offer)**
*   Sense (2) - for consumer PM roles
*   Domain (8) - for specialist roles (Ads PM, ML PM, etc.)
*   Technical (7) - for infra-heavy products

**Support Pillars (Should be "good enough")**
*   Metrics (4)
*   Management (5)
