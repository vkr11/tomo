2021 was a challenging year for so many people. The level of uncertainty in the world from the pandemic, the changing nature of work, and the shifts in family life have made us rethink our careers and what work means to us. As we exit this year and enter a new one, I’ve asked five incredible leadership and executive coaches for their advice on how to end 2021 well and start 2022 on the right foot. Most of these are coaches I respect and recommended in a previous post called [Career Coaching: How an Outside Perspective Can Change Your Life](https://debliu.substack.com/p/career-coaching-how-an-outside-perspective).
